Features

Wireless Charging:

AirPods come with a wireless charging case that can be charged using a Qi-compatible charging mat or Lightning connector. This convenient feature ensures that your AirPods are always ready for your next adventure.

Siri Integration:

Activate Siri with a simple "Hey Siri" command, allowing you to control your music, make calls, ask questions, and more all without reaching for your device. AirPods provide a hands-free and intuitive way to interact with your digital assistant.

Force Sensor

To play and pause audio, press the force sensor on the stem of an AirPod. To resume playback, press again. To skip forwards, double-press the force sensor. To skip back, triple-press the force sensor

Seamless iCloud Integration:

AirPods seamlessly integrate with your iCloud account, making pairing and switching between your Apple devices effortless. iCloud also keeps your AirPods in sync, so you can easily switch between devices while continuing to enjoy your audio.
